How It Works

Set it up once.
Let it run.

No complex onboarding. Add creators, configure your thresholds, and the platform handles everything else.

Add your creators

Paste an Instagram handle. ViralTrack pulls their profile, follower count, and post history automatically. Group them by niche or client.
